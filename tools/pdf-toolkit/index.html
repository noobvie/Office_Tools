About PDF Toolkit

This is your all-in-one browser-based PDF workspace. Merge multiple PDFs and images into a single document, split a PDF to extract specific pages, or compress an oversized PDF to a smaller file size — all without anything leaving your device. No Adobe Acrobat subscription, no paid tools, no uploads to a cloud service.

Perfect for office workers, students, accountants, and freelancers who regularly need to combine invoices and supporting documents into one file, pull out a few pages from a large report, or shrink PDFs before emailing them to clients. Fast, private, and free — the way tools should be.

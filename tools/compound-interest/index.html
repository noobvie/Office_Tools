About this tool

See the power of compounding. Enter your starting principal, an annual interest rate, how long you'll invest, and any regular contribution. The calculator compounds at your chosen frequency and shows your future balance, total contributions, and total interest earned, plus a year-by-year growth chart and breakdown table.

Contributions are assumed at the start of each compounding period. All maths runs locally — results are estimates and do not account for tax, fees, or inflation.

Turn titles into clean, SEO-friendly URL slugs. Single or bulk, with smart options.

About this tool

A good URL slug is lowercase, readable, and free of spaces or special characters. Paste a title and get a tidy SEO-friendly slug: accents are transliterated (café → cafe), punctuation is stripped, and words are joined with hyphens. Options let you remove common stop words (a, the, of…), switch to underscores, or cap the length.

Enter multiple lines to generate slugs in bulk — one per line. Everything runs locally in your browser.

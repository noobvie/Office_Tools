About this converter

US recipes measure by volume (cups, tablespoons) while most of the world measures dry ingredients by weight (grams). You can't convert between them with a single number — it depends on the ingredient's density. A cup of all-purpose flour is ~120 g but a cup of granulated sugar is ~200 g. Pick your ingredient and this tool uses the right density for each conversion.

Below the converter there's also a recipe scaler to multiply or divide every quantity when you change the number of servings.

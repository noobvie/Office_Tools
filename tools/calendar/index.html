About World Calendar

This World Calendar lets you view any month across three calendar systems side by side: the Gregorian calendar used internationally, the Chinese Lunar calendar (公历), and the Islamic Hijri calendar. Click any date to get a full comparison of the corresponding date in all three systems at once.

Perfect for people who need to coordinate across cultures and traditions — whether you're planning around Chinese New Year, Islamic holidays, or converting a Gregorian date into a Lunar equivalent for family events. Great for multicultural families, international teams, and the curious.

About — Chinese Lunar dates use the traditional lunisolar calendar (农历) including leap months (閏月). Islamic Hijri dates use the civil tabular calendar. All conversions run locally in your browser — no data is sent anywhere.

About Image Cropper

Cut an image down to exactly the region you want. Drag the crop box, resize the handles, and use the aspect-ratio presets (1:1 square for profile pictures, 16:9 for thumbnails, 4:3, 3:2, and free-form) to lock proportions. You can also rotate, flip, and zoom before exporting as JPG, PNG, or WebP.

The crop is rendered at the image's full original resolution, not the on-screen preview size, so you keep maximum quality. Processing happens entirely in your browser using the open-source Cropper.js library — your photo is never uploaded to any server. Perfect for avatars, banners, passport photos, and trimming screenshots.

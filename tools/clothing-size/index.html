About this converter

Buying shoes or clothes from another country? Sizing systems differ wildly — a US women's 8 is a UK 6 and an EU 39, and clothing labels jump from numbers (US/UK) to centimetres (EU) to letters (S/M/L). This tool lays out the standard women's and men's shoe and clothing charts side by side and highlights your equivalent size across every region.

Sizes vary by brand and country — these charts use the most common conversions. When you're between sizes or buying a fitted item, check the retailer's own size guide and measure in cm where possible.

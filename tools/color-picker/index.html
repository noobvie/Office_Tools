🎨 Color Picker / Eyedropper from Image

Pick any color from an image with a magnified loupe. Get HEX, RGB and HSL, and save a palette.

About this tool

Upload an image and hover to magnify, then click to grab the exact pixel color. You get the value in HEX, RGB and HSL, a large preview swatch, and one-click copy. Keep clicking to build a palette of saved colors — handy for matching brand colors, sampling a photo, or recreating a design.

Everything is processed locally with the canvas API; your image is never uploaded.

About Sign PDF

Add a signature to a PDF without printing, scanning or uploading to a third party. Draw your signature with a mouse or finger, type it in a handwriting style, or upload an image of your signature. Then drag it onto the page, resize it, and download the flattened, signed PDF. Everything happens locally — your document never leaves your device.

Note: this places a visible signature image (a simple e-signature). It is not a cryptographic digital signature / certificate.

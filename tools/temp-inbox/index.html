About Temp Inbox

A temporary inbox gives you a throwaway email address you can hand to a website to confirm a signup, grab a one-time code, or test your own app's emails — without exposing your real address or risking spam. Click New address, copy it, use it, then watch this page for incoming mail. The address (and everything sent to it) is deleted automatically after the time shown.

Anyone who knows the address can read its mail, so never use it for anything private or for password resets on accounts you care about. Messages are shown in a sandboxed frame with scripts disabled. If your message doesn't appear, give it a minute and hit Refresh — delivery depends on the sender. To check whether an address is disposable rather than create one, use the Disposable Email Detector.

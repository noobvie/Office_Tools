About this calculator

Fill in when you started and finished each day and how many minutes of unpaid break you took. The tool adds up hours worked per day and for the whole week, handles shifts that run past midnight, and splits the total into regular and overtime hours (over 40/week at 1.5×). Add an hourly rate to see your gross pay. Everything stays in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

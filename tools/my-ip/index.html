About What Is My IP

Sometimes you just need a quick answer: what is my public IP address right now? Whether you're setting up a server firewall rule, troubleshooting network connectivity, configuring remote access, or checking whether your VPN is actually working — knowing your public IP is the first step. This tool shows it instantly, along with additional network information like your ISP and rough location.

Your public IP is what the rest of the internet sees when you make requests — different from your local/private IP assigned by your router. Useful for IT admins, developers, remote workers, and anyone doing network setup or security checks who needs this information fast without ads or distractions.
